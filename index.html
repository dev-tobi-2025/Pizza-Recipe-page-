Simple omelette recipes

An easy and quick dish perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese vegetables or meats

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10minutes

  • Preparation:5minutes

  • Cooking:5minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3large eggs

  • Salt, to taste

  • Pepper, to taste

  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil

  • Optional fillings: cheese, diceds vegetables, cooked meat

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs :in a bowl beat the egg with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer solution

  2. Heat the pan :Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil

  3. Cook the omlette :Once the butter is meamt and bubbling, pour in the eggs tilt the pan to ensure the egg evenly coat the surface

  4. Add fillings(optional) : when the eggs begin to set at the edge
